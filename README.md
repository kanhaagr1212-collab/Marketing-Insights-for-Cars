# Marketing-Insights-for-Cars

Insight on Graphs:

1.BMW has the highest average car price among all brands.

2.Ford has the lowest average price, making it the most affordable brand in the dataset.

3.Newer manufacturing years generally show higher car prices.

4.Hybrid cars have the highest average price, Petrol and diesel cars have relatively moderate average prices.

5.Automatic cars are more expensive than manual cars, Higher comfort and demand increase the price of automatic cars.

6.Toyota Corolla and Ford Fiesta are the most common models in the dataset.

7.Popular sedan and SUV models dominate the dataset.

8.Brands with larger engine sizes generally have higher prices, BMW has both high engine size and high average price.
Honda and Toyota maintain moderate engine sizes with balanced pricing.
